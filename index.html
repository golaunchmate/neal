What is the Neal Test?

The Neal Test is a lean startup validation methodology that proves customer demand before you build technology. Instead of spending months developing software, you manually deliver the core service and get paid for it.

The Two Non-Negotiable Components:

  1. Manual Service Delivery: You personally perform the work WITHOUT any technology or automation
  2. Customer Payment: They pay you for this manual service

What This Tool Does

The run_neal_test_session tool generates 5 personalized Neal Test scenarios for any startup idea. Each scenario includes:

  • Specific manual service approach (no tech required)
  • Concrete payment method
  • What you'll learn from the experiment
  • Step-by-step execution plan
